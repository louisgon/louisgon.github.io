About Me

Hello, I'm Louis Gonzalez - a Front-End Developer from Dallas, Texas. I have worked on the web for over 8 years, working on 200+ projects.

Making websites started as a passionate hobby for me in 2010. From creating funky custom themes for my hobbies, to experimenting with a Social Media project with a friend. After making a little bit of money I decided to withdraw myself from college and pursue my dream of making websites.

Starting in 2013 I began freelancing, which changed my life. I began working with various companies, including the one I am currently employed at. Working on over 150+ projects mostly independent. I even got to collaborate with some major brands.

Fast-forward to today, and I'm currently employed at a Digital Marketing Agency. For the last year I've been making one of a kind web applications, focusing on clean, efficient, modular coding standards on over 20+ projects to deliver a satisfying user experience.
