About Me

Hello, I'm Louis Gonzalez - a Front-End Developer from Dallas, Texas. I have worked on the web for over 8 years, working on 200+ projects.

Making websites started as a passionate hobby for me in 2010, from creating funky custom themes for my hobbies to experimenting with a Social Media project with a friend. After making consistent money, I decided to withdraw from college and pursue my passion for creating websites.

Starting in 2013, I began freelancing, which changed my life. I began collaborating with various agencies, including Calibrate Marketing, independently working on front-end development for 150+ projects. I even got to collaborate with some key brands during this period.

Fast-forward to today, I'm employed at a Digital Marketing Agency. For the last year, I've been making one-of-a-kind web applications, focusing on clean, efficient, modular coding standards on over 20+ projects to deliver a satisfying user experience.
